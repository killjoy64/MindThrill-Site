FAQs

At Mind Thrill escape rooms, we want to make sure you have the most enjoyable experience possible. In order to do so, we are willing to adjust the game to your needs.

It is very possible to escape! We plan our rooms to be challenging and full of creative puzzles, but also doable for the average group!

We recommend groups of at least 2, and no more than 8.

The total process should take no longer than an hour and a half. Arrive at least 15 minutes early.

Our Tardis room is a play on the show Doctor Who, and is a mini escape room with monthly puzzles.

If you're late to your reservation, your time slot becomes available for anyone to take.
