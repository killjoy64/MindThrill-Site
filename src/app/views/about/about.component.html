You walk into a normal room themed to the game. At that moment, you are locked inside and must escape!

You have 60 minutes (1 hour) to escape the room. Three free hints are available to you, but any hints beyond that will be costly to your adventure.

Each room is unique with puzzles fitting to it's theme. Work together to uncover clues and solve complex puzzles pertinent to your escape.

You don't have to go alone! Bring friends or family to help aid you in your escape. We recommend anywhere between 4-8 players in a group.
